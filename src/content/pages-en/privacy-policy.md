## Data Collected and Purposes

### Site Browsing

Browsing izybiz.fr does not require providing personal data. Technical data collected automatically (IP address, browser type, pages visited, session duration) is used solely for anonymised statistical purposes, in the publisher's legitimate interest (site optimisation and security). This data is retained for a maximum of 12 months.

### Contact Form

Data submitted via the contact form (name, email, message) is used exclusively to respond to your enquiry. It is not shared with third parties. Retention period: 3 years from the last contact.

### Newsletter

If you subscribe to the newsletter, your email address is collected on the basis of your explicit consent. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in each email or by contacting us directly. Your data is deleted promptly after unsubscription.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of access**: obtain a copy of your data
- **Right to rectification**: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to erasure**: request deletion of your data
- **Right to restriction**: limit the processing of your data
- **Right to object**: object to the processing of your data
- **Right to portability**: receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, use the [contact form](/contact) and specify your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the CNIL ([www.cnil.fr](https://www.cnil.fr)) if you believe your data is not being processed in accordance with applicable regulations.
